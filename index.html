How to Fight Negativity Around Us?

“A superhero is demonstrated in the comic book panel revealing his super powers. In the adjacent demonstration, daughter of the superhero asks her mom where is daddy doing? The mother replies “to stop negativity Child!”. So this demonstrates how every one should take part in stopping negativity around us.

Notice that many of our actions and those of others are intended to relieve suffering or bring happiness. (When you encounter a painful stimulus, you withdraw from it.) Recognizing that this is basic to all human beings provides a deep sense of interconnection with those around you and cultivates an appreciation that we all wish to be happy and to relieve suffering.”
